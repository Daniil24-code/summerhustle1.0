02 — How Each Job Goes

The 5-Step
Job Flow

01
Clean the Driveway
Sweep with brooms, blow out all debris with air blower. Surface must be clear before any product goes down.
~15 min
02
Fill the Cracks
Apply crack filler to any gaps or splits in the asphalt. Let set before proceeding. This is what separates a pro job from a rush job.
~10 min
03
Tape Off the Interlock
Mask the edges — interlock, grass line, garage door threshold. Clean lines = happy customers = referrals.
~10 min
04
Apply Asphalt Sealer
Spray the driveway evenly with asphalt sealer. Work in sections, keep it uniform. This is the money shot — the finish that makes driveways look brand new.
~25 min
05
Block Off + Offer Window Clean
Cone off the driveway, give the client a dry time heads-up. While it dries, offer window cleaning. No extra travel, pure added revenue.
